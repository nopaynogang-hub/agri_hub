About Sokoine University of Agriculture

University History

Founded in 1984, SUA is Tanzania's premier agricultural university located in Morogoro.

College of Natural and Applied Sciences

Home to the Department of Informatics and Information Technology.

Research Excellence

Leading research institution in agriculture, food security, and environmental sciences.

Our History

AgriSolutions Hub was conceptualized in 2024 as an educational project by David Bablo Mushii, a Second Year Diploma student in Information Technology at Sokoine University of Agriculture.

The project aims to bridge the digital divide in Tanzania's agricultural sector by providing farmers with technology-driven solutions for market access, information sharing, and business growth.

Our Mission

To democratize global agricultural trade by providing smallholder farmers and agribusinesses with the tools, information, and connections they need to succeed in international markets.

Our Vision

To create a world where every farmer has equal access to global markets, fair prices, and sustainable business opportunities, fostering economic growth in agricultural communities worldwide.

Our Values

Transparency, sustainability, innovation, and partnership guide every decision we make. We believe in creating long-term value for all stakeholders in the agricultural value chain.
